Digital Marketer Intern

Employment Context

Cultivo develops solutions to real world problems in the supply chain sector that bring transparency, efficiency, and trust to end users and the logistic ecosystem. In addition to our SaaS commodity management solutions, we are exploring opportunities such as freight management,and artificial Intelligence Integration in Supply Chain Analytics

Position Purpose

We are looking for a prolific and talented digital marketer to write engaging content, manage social media, and execute email campaigns to expand our company’s digital footprint, awareness, subscribers, and leads. This role requires a high level of creativity, attention to detail, and project management skills.This role is a fixed-term, paid internship with potential to progress within Cultivo upon completion of the internship.

Goals include:

attracting site traffic and social followers, driving new sales leads, empowering sales and support with relevant content, and fueling media and stakeholder relations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Build, manage, and execute a rich content/editorial calendar that attracts a qualified audience (including blog posts, whitepapers, ebooks, reports, etc.)
  • Build, manage, and segment various email lists and campaigns, including the design templates, calls-to-action, and content
  • Manage our social media profiles and presence.
  • Establish closed-loop analytics with sales and support to understand and iteratively improve our marketing and sales activities
  • Manage both on-site and off-site SEO for the company, as well as organic search engine performance and goal setting based on site impressions, clickthrough rates, traffic, and conversions would be an add on if known
  • Convince others that your creative ideas are worth investing time and effort in-this role is at the core of the marketing team, and others will rely on your work every single day
  • Support communications with media and other external stakeholders
  • Investigate and trial the latest digital marketing and customer experience technologies

Qualifications, Skills & Experience

  • Proven and visible marketer with experience creating content for the web across several disciplines (text, design, video, etc.) and media (blogging, guides whitepapers, research, infographics, podcasts, etc.).
  • Social media expert
  • Excellent writer and communicator, able to lead and communicate cross-functionally with engineering and other departments, and externally across stakeholders and customers with engaging and thought-provoking content
  • Self-starter who is willing to take accountability for deliverables and consistently exceed expectations
  • Creative and strategic thinker, driven by intuition but able to gut check and recorrect with data, as well as report results and optimize using data
  • Team player, willing to work collaboratively in a high-energy, fast-moving, startup environment
  • Enthusiastic for and experience in the supply chain industry a plus

Location:

Bangalore

Reports To:

Business Development Head
