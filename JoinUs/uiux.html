UI/UX Intern

Employment Context

Cultivo develops solutions to real world problems in the supply chain sector that bring transparency, efficiency, and trust to end users and the logistic ecosystem. In addition to our SaaS commodity management solutions, we are exploring opportunities such as freight management,and artificial Intelligence Integration in Supply Chain Analytics

Position Purpose

We are looking for an efficient, highly organised and proactive UI/UXIntern to fill a critical role supporting our Tech Team. At Cultivo we have a fast-growing team of developers and supply chain enthusiasts and are looking for an ambitious and talented individual to help us with our next stage of growth. The purpose of this role is to provide support to the digital side of our business in the day-to-day running of Cultivo. This role requires a high level of creativity, attention to detail, and project management skills.This role is a fixed-term, with potential to progress within Cultivo upon completion of the internship.

Key Responsibilities

  • Overall responsibility for understanding a customer's brief and converting that into appealing, highly usable interfaces.
  • Engages with the business development team and/or customer early on to seek a deeper appreciation of the customers’ business problem/goals.
  • Develops understanding of the end users of the website/application through secondary and primary research (user interviews, traffic data).
  • Capable of generating user personas and user scenarios.
  • Prepares low fidelity prototypes of sites/features (ranging from paper-and-pencil concepts to wireframes or interactive prototypes) for internal review and brainstorming.
  • Capable of gauging technical feasibility of the prototype. Presents high fidelity designs/mock-ups to the customer for review/iteration/approval.
  • Capable of working directly with customers in an iterative/agile manner to dramatically improve the prototypes in a short time
  • Able to explain his/her design philosophy verbally and in writing

Qualifications, Skills & Experience

  • Solid portfolio demonstrating design savvy.
  • Good eye for aesthetics: layout, grid systems, color theory and typography.
  • Strong understanding of mobile platforms, particularly iOS, Android .
  • Understanding of the limitations of web and mobile, with approaches/ideas to flex those boundaries.
  • Candidates must absolutely be in sync with recent design standards and trends.
  • Formal education in interaction design (Bachelor's/Master's/Diploma) is a definite plus.
  • Excellent writer and communicator, able to lead and communicate cross-functionally with engineering and other departments, and externally across stakeholders and customers with engaging and thought-provoking content
  • Self-starter
  • Creative and strategic thinker, driven by intuition but able to gut check and redirect with data, as well as report results and optimize using research
  • Team player, willing to work collaboratively in a high-energy, fast-moving, startup environment
  • Enthusiastic for and experience in the supply chain industry a plus
